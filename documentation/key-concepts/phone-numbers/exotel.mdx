## Overview

Importing your Exotel phone numbers into Cekura allows you to:

- **Test inbound calls** using your existing Exotel numbers
- **Run outbound tests** from your Exotel numbers
- **Maintain your existing phone infrastructure** while leveraging Cekura's testing capabilities
- **Access detailed call logs and metrics** from your Exotel-based tests

<Info>
  **BYOP (Bring Your Own Phone):** This feature allows you to use your existing Exotel phone numbers without purchasing new numbers from Cekura.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- An active **Exotel account** with at least one phone number
- Your Exotel **API Key**
- Your Exotel **API Token**
- Your Exotel **Account SID**
- Your **Subdomain** (based on your region)
- A phone number purchased in your Exotel account

<Note>
  If you don't have an Exotel phone number, you can purchase one from the [Exotel Dashboard](https://my.exotel.com/).
</Note>

## Import Your Exotel Number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Retrieve Exotel Credentials">
    1. Log in to the [Exotel Dashboard](https://my.exotel.com/).
    2. Navigate to **Settings** → **API Settings**.
    3. Copy your **API Key**, **API Token**, and **Account SID**.
    4. Note your **Subdomain** based on your region:
       - `api.exotel.com` for Singapore
       - `api.in.exotel.com` for Mumbai (India)

    <Warning>
      Keep your API Key and API Token secure and never share them publicly. They provide full access to your Exotel account.
    </Warning>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Your Phone Number">
    1. In the Exotel Dashboard, go to **Phone Numbers**.
    2. Select the phone number you want to import.
    3. Copy the phone number in E.164 format (e.g., `+914044449444`).

  </Step>

  <Step title="Import to Cekura">
    1. Log in to your [Cekura Dashboard](https://dashboard.cekura.ai).
    2. Navigate to **Settings** → **Phone Numbers**.
    3. Click **Import Phone Number**.
    4. Select **Exotel** as the provider.
    5. Fill in the required details:
       - **Phone Number**: Enter your Exotel number in E.164 format
       - **API Key**: Paste your Exotel API Key
       - **API Token**: Paste your Exotel API Token
       - **Account SID**: Paste your Exotel Account SID
       - **Subdomain**: Select your region-specific subdomain
       - **Friendly Name** (Optional): Add a label for easy identification
    6. Click **Import Number**.

    <Tip>
      Use descriptive friendly names like "Support Line" or "Sales Demo" to easily identify numbers in your dashboard.
    </Tip>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Import">
    Once imported, your Exotel number will appear in the **Phone Numbers** list with:
    - Phone number
    - Provider (Exotel)
    - Status (Active)
    - Friendly name

    <Check>
      Your Exotel number is now ready to use with Cekura for testing!
    </Check>

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Your Imported Number

### For Inbound Testing

1. Navigate to **Agents** → Select your agent
2. In **Agent Settings**, set the imported Exotel number as the **Contact Number**
3. Cekura will automatically route test calls to your agent via Exotel

### For Outbound Testing

1. When creating an **Evaluator** or running tests
2. Select your imported Exotel number as the **Caller ID**
3. Outbound test calls will originate from your Exotel number

## Regional Subdomains

Exotel uses different subdomains based on your account's region:

| Region | Subdomain |
| ------ | --------- |
| Singapore | `api.exotel.com` |
| Mumbai (India) | `api.in.exotel.com` |

<Note>
  Make sure to select the correct subdomain for your Exotel account region. Using the wrong subdomain will result in authentication errors.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Import Failed: Invalid Credentials">
    **Solution:**
    - Verify your API Key, API Token, and Account SID are correct
    - Ensure you're using the correct subdomain for your region
    - Check that your Exotel account is active and in good standing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Phone Number Already Imported">
    **Solution:**
    - Each Exotel number can only be imported once per Cekura project
    - Check if the number is already in your **Phone Numbers** list
    - Contact support if you need to update an existing import
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Calls Not Routing Correctly">
    **Solution:**
    - Ensure the phone number is in E.164 format (e.g., `+914044449444`)
    - Verify your Exotel number has voice capabilities enabled
    - Check Exotel Dashboard for any account restrictions or errors
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong Subdomain Error">
    **Solution:**
    - Singapore accounts should use `api.exotel.com`
    - Mumbai/India accounts should use `api.in.exotel.com`
    - Check your Exotel account settings to confirm your region
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
