## What You'll Learn

How Cekura billing works, how to monitor your credit balance, and how to avoid running out of credits unexpectedly.

## Credits System

Cekura uses a credit-based billing model. Every call simulation, metric evaluation, and API operation consumes credits. Your current balance is visible in the dashboard under **Settings → Billing**.

## Set Up Low Credits Alerts

Get notified before you run out.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Organization Settings">
    Go to **Settings → Organization → General** in the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Notifications">
    Toggle **Low Credits Notifications** to ON.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Threshold">
    Enter a threshold (default: 1,000 credits). You'll receive an email when your balance drops below this number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Set a threshold that gives you at least a day of runway based on your typical usage. Combine with **auto-refill** in billing settings to automatically top up credits.
</Tip>

## Best Practices

- **Keep admin emails current** — only org admins receive low-credit alerts
- **Use auto-refill** — prevents service interruptions from an empty balance
- **Monitor usage per project** — helps identify which agents or test suites are consuming the most credits
