## Prerequisites

- A Cekura account
- Retell API credentials

## Setting Up Retell Integration

<Steps>
<Step title="Access Organization Settings">
Log in to your [Cekura Dashboard](https://dashboard.cekura.ai), choose the **Organization** you want to configure Retell for, and click on the **Settings** button in the header.

<img src="/images/org-button-click.png" alt="Organization Settings Navigation" />
</Step>

<Step title="Configure Retell Authentication">
Locate the **Provider API Keys** section in the sidebar, enter your **Retell API key** in the designated field, and click on **Save Changes** button.

<img src="/images/retell/retell-api-setup.png" alt="Retell API Configuration" />
</Step>

<Step title="Create a New Agent">
Navigate to the **Agents** dropdown from the sidebar, click on **Create an Agent** button, fill in the required details in the form, and click **Create** button.

<img src="/images/create-agent.png" alt="Create Agent" />
<img src="/images/create-agent-modal.png" alt="Creating Agent" />
</Step>

<Step title="Select Retell as Your Provider">
After creating the agent, you will be redirected to the agent's settings page. Choose **Retell** from the **Provider** dropdown menu, copy your agent ID from Retell into the Assistant ID field, and click on **Save Changes** button.

<img src="/images/choosing-agent-provider.png" alt="Choosing Agent Provider" />
</Step>
</Steps>
