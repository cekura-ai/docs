## Prerequisites

- A Cekura account
- ElevenLabs API credentials

## Setting Up ElevenLabs Integration

<Steps>
<Step title="Access Organization Settings">
Log in to your [Cekura Dashboard](https://dashboard.cekura.ai), choose the **Organization** you want to configure ElevenLabs for, and click on the **Settings** button in the header.

<img src="/images/org-button-click.png" alt="Organization Settings Navigation" />
</Step>

<Step title="Configure ElevenLabs Authentication">
Locate the **Provider API Keys** or **ElevenLabs Integration** section in the sidebar, enter your **ElevenLabs API key** in the designated field, and click **Save Changes**.

<img src="/images/elevenlabs-api-key.png" alt="ElevenLabs API Configuration" />
</Step>

<Step title="Create a New Agent">
Navigate to the **Agents** dropdown from the sidebar, click on **Create an Agent**, fill in the required details, and click **Create**.

<img src="/images/create-agent.png" alt="Create Agent" />
</Step>

<Step title="Select ElevenLabs as Your Provider">
When editing the agent, choose **ElevenLabs** from the **Provider** dropdown, copy your Assistant/Agent ID from ElevenLabs into the Assistant ID field, and save your changes.

<img src="/images/create-agent.png" alt="Choosing Agent Provider" />
</Step>
</Steps>

## Testing Your Agent

<Steps>
<Step title="Run a Test Call">
Make a test call (phone or webcall) to your ElevenLabs-backed agent. Verify the call appears in the Cekura Calls dashboard and that transcripts are populated.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify Transcript and Audio">
Ensure the transcript type is set to `elevenlabs` and confirm audio files are available in the call log entry.
</Step>
</Steps>
