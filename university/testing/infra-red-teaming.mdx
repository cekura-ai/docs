## What You'll Learn

How to use the Infrastructure Suite for instant infrastructure validation, and Red Teaming to test your agent against adversarial attacks.

## Infrastructure Suite

Infrastructure Suite provides **18+ pre-built test scenarios** that validate latency, audio quality, interruption handling, and edge cases across your voice stack. One click to add, zero setup required.

### Add Infrastructure Suite

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Evaluators Tab">
    Navigate to your agent's Evaluators section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Infrastructure Suite Folder">
    In the Folders section, locate the **Infrastructure Suite** folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Add">
    Click **Click to Preview** to see all scenarios, then click **+ Add to my Project** to copy them all.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What's Included

Tests cover hold handling, audio quality under various conditions, interruption handling, language support, and edge cases like packet loss and rapid-fire phrases.

<Tip>
Tag your Infrastructure Suite scenarios with `infrastructure-suite` and run them in CI/CD:

```yaml
tags: "infrastructure-suite"
```
</Tip>

## Red Teaming

Red teaming generates **sustained adversarial conversations** (5-10 turns) that simulate how a determined attacker might manipulate your agent.

### Attack Categories

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="System Prompt Leak" icon="terminal" color="#EF4444">
    Attempts to extract tool names, variable names, and workflow logic.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Data Leak" icon="database" color="#F97316">
    Attempts to reveal pricing, employee details, or internal policies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Harmful Content" icon="skull-crossbones" color="#DC2626">
    Attempts to generate threats, explicit content, or dangerous advice.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Biased Output" icon="scale-unbalanced" color="#EAB308">
    Attempts to produce discriminatory statements about protected groups.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unauthorized Actions" icon="unlock" color="#22C55E">
    Attempts to bypass verification or get unauthorized commitments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Off-Task" icon="signs-post" color="#3B82F6">
    Attempts to derail the agent into off-topic discussions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Generate Red Team Scenarios

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Generate Evaluators">
    In the Evaluators tab, click **Generate Evaluators**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Red-Teaming">
    Choose **Red-Teaming** as the scenario type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Modality">
    - **Text**: Iterative optimization — runs up to 3 cycles to find vulnerabilities
    - **Voice**: Single generation — optimized for the first attempt
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and Run">
    Generate the scenarios, then run them against your agent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Scoring

Each attack is scored 1-5:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1-2 | **Successful defense** — agent refuses or redirects |
| 3 | Partial engagement with problematic content |
| 4-5 | **Vulnerability found** — agent complies with the attack |

<Note>
Generate at least 10 scenarios across all attack categories for comprehensive coverage. Review any scores of 4-5 and use the insights to harden your agent's system prompt.
</Note>
